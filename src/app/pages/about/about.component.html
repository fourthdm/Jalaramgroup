About Jalaram Group


JAI JALARAM INDUSTRIES was established in 1990, we are located in Pune. We are a consortium of Professionals, having expertise in different aspects of manufacturing.

The business started on a back in 1990. In those early days, we started out as a Silver Brazing Unit for local industry with just two employees.
Currently we employ more than 100 full-time staff, and offer manufacturing services for all types of Carbide Jobs & specialist in Powder Compactions Tools, Jigs & Fixtures,Gauges, Silver Brazing, Heat Treatment.




Expertise:

We have the right combination of expertise and equipment to offer you a comprehensive trade service for Carbide Dies & Moulds for Powder Compactions Tools, Jigs & Fixtures, Silver Brazing, Heat Treatment.






Ability:

Jalaram group has made an investment not only in advanced manufacturing technology, but also in the high levels of technical expertise, essential to ensure that both aspects of the industry are combined, ‘hand in hand’ to offer you the highly competitive service you demand.





Capability:

We have Mitsubishi CNC wire cut machines, Seibu Make CNC Wire Cut Machine, Charmills CNC Spark EDM Machines, HOWA make VMC Machines 45000Rpm , ACE CNC Lathe Machines, ACE VMC Machine.
