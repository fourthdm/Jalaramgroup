Growth is Life


JAI JALARAM INDUSTRIES was established in 1990. We are located in Pune. We are a consortium of professionals with expertise in different aspects of manufacturing.

The business started back in 1990. In those early days, we started out as a silver brazing unit for local industry with just two employees. Currently we employ more than 100 full-time staff and offer manufacturing services for all types of carbide jobs and specialize in powder compaction tools, jigs & fixtures, gauges, silver brazing, and heat treatment.
