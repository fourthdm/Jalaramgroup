Welcome To

Jalaram Group


Jalaram Group is a group of Industries which owns JAI JALARAM INDUSTRIES, VEER ENTERPRISES, MAYUR ENTERPRISES, RIYA ENTERPRISES & J SQUARE INDUSTRIES.

Jalaram Group was formed in the year 1990 in Pune by Mr. Rameshwar Bhagat. We have the right combination of expertise and equipment to offer you a comprehensive trade service for carbide dies & moulds for powder compaction tools, jigs & fixtures, silver brazing, and heat treatment.













What We Design ?

We have professional and hands-on experienced designers who are well trained in CAD, CAM, and mould designing software. Our designers understand the needs of clients and design as per clients' satisfaction, which helps further manufacturing process easy and accurate.

What We Manufacture ?

We have well equipped and advanced CNC, EDM, and VMC imported machines that are regularly serviced and well maintained by our professionals. We maintain sufficient raw materials and tools in stocks, which helps us to deliver quality products and quality tested materials in time.

What We Deliver ?

We have a special team that manages to deliver all requested orders in time. All our products are well tested and cross-checked by our experienced delivery team, which avoids errors in delivering products. We have our own transport facilities, which help us to avoid delay in transportation.
